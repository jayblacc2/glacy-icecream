Our Sweet Story

From a small family kitchen to your favorite ice cream destination — discover the passion behind every scoop

Since 2015

Crafting Happiness, One Scoop at a Time

Glacy Store began with a simple dream: to create ice cream that brings pure joy to every bite. What started as a small family kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved destination for ice cream lovers across California.

Our founder, inspired by traditional Italian gelato techniques and a passion for natural ingredients, set out to create something truly special. Today, we continue that legacy by handcrafting each batch with the same love and attention to detail.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Premium Quality

We use only the finest natural ingredients, sourcing cream and milk from local farms to ensure every scoop is perfect.

100% Natural

No artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives. Just pure, honest ingredients that you can taste in every bite.

Farm Fresh

Direct partnerships with local dairy farms ensure our ingredients are always fresh and sustainably sourced.

Perfect Delivery

Our specialized thermo-packaging keeps your ice cream at the perfect temperature from our shop to your door.

Our Journey

Milestones That Matter

2015

The Beginning

Glacy Store opened its first small shop on Broadway Street with just 12 flavors and a dream.

2017

Expanding Flavors

Introduced our signature fruit sherbets and reached 30 unique flavors in our collection.

2019

Going Online

Launched our e-commerce platform to bring joy to customers across the state with home delivery.

2022

Eco-Friendly

2025

Sweet Future

Continuing to innovate with new flavors while staying true to our roots of quality and passion.

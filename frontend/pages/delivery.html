Temperature Guarantee

Our specialized thermo-packaging keeps your ice cream at the perfect temperature (-10°F to -5°F) from our shop to your door. We use:

  • Premium insulated boxes
  • Dry ice pellets
  • Thermal liners
  • Temperature monitoring
